01 · Connectivity Resilience

Networks built for organisations that cannot afford to go dark.

Downtime isn't just an inconvenience — it's lost revenue, lost clients, and lost trust. nunu designs and operates connectivity infrastructure with redundancy baked in at every layer, not added as an afterthought.

From SD-WAN and multi-carrier failover through to private circuit design, your connectivity is engineered to keep your business running when the unexpected happens.

  • Real-time network health monitoring and alerting
  • Dedicated and leased line options for critical sites
  • 4G/5G backup as standard on resilience plans
  • Quality of service prioritisation for voice and video

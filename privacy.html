Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Who we are

nunu advanced technologies LTD ("nunu", "we", "our") is a UK-registered company providing unified communications, connectivity, security and AI services to businesses. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you use our website at nunu.solutions.

What data we collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number and company name (when you submit a form)
  • Information you provide in message fields
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type and IP address
  • Usage data including pages visited and time spent on site

How we use your data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and requests
  • Deliver the Clarity Audit service you have requested
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will not use your data for marketing purposes unless you have given us explicit consent to do so.

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent - when you submit a form, you consent to us processing your data for that purpose
  • Legitimate interest - to improve our website and services
  • Legal obligation - where we are required by law to retain certain data

Data retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. Form submissions are retained for up to 24 months unless you request deletion sooner.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website and delivering our services, subject to appropriate data processing agreements. All data is stored within the UK or EEA.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at intel@nunu.solutions.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only to ensure the site functions correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
